The Art of Custom Laser-Cut Commissions

While our standard collection offers hundreds of challenges, sometimes a solver seeks something truly unique. Our custom commission program allows individuals and corporations to bring their own visions to life in wood.

From Sketch to Reality

The process begins with a consultation. Whether it's a mechanical representation of a family heirloom or a corporate gift that reflects a company's architecture, our design team works closely with the client to ensure the logic and aesthetic are perfect.

"A custom puzzle is not just a gift; it's a legacy piece that tells a specific story."

We've created everything from laser-cut wedding invitation boxes with hidden compartments to massive 1,000-piece mechanical wall installations. If you can dream it, we can cut it.
