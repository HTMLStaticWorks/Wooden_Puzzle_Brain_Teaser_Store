Difficulty Guide

Find the perfect challenge level for your skill set.

01

Beginner (The Curious)

Perfect for children or those new to mechanical puzzles. Typically involves 3-8 pieces with intuitive logic. Solving time: 15-30 minutes.

02

Intermediate (The Enthusiast)

Requires spatial reasoning and a few "Aha!" moments. 10-25 pieces. Solving time: 1-2 hours.

03

Expert (The Master)

Complex sequences and hidden locks. 25-50 pieces. Requires patience and strategic thinking. Solving time: 4-6 hours.

04

Legendary (The Grandmaster)

Our most intricate designs. Often involves 100+ precision pieces or complex mechanical gears. Solving time: Days or weeks.
