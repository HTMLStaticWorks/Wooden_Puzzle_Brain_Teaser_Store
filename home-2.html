Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our artisan puzzles.

Are the puzzles difficult to solve?

We offer a range of difficulty levels from Beginner to Legendary. Each puzzle page includes a rating to help you choose the right challenge.

What type of wood do you use?

We primarily use American Black Walnut and Sugar Maple, sourced from sustainable forests in Vermont and Canada.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our handcrafted puzzles to enthusiasts all over the world. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout.
